Ochazuke

Back

Description

Ochazuke is a quick, easy and delicious way to use up leftover rice. It's also a nice fast meal when you're too exhausted to cook. Using just rice, hot tea, and the toppins of your choice, you'll have a meal ready in no time! Feel free to experiment with different toppings, such as different types of fish, vegetables, pickles, and seasonings. This recipe calls for a traditional topping of salmon flakes and nori.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups leftover white rice
  • 1/4 cup salmon flakes (or dried fish, such as katsuobushi)
  • 1 to 2 tsp shredded nori
  • 1 to 2 leaves shiso, cut into strips
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds, toasted
  • 1 cup green tea

Steps

  1. Place mound of rice in a bowl
  2. Arrange salmon or dried fish on rice
  3. Add shredded nori, sesame seeds and shiso
  4. Pour tea over other ingredients. Enjoy!
