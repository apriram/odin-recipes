Cucumber Salad

Back

Description

Cucumber salad is a light and refreshing side dish or snack for hot summer days.

Only 5 to 10 minutes of prep work is required, and the results are super satisfying!

Ingredients

  • One large English cucumber, or 2-3 regular cucumbers (peeled)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp sugar
  • 2 Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds (optional)
  1. Slice cucumber thinly.
  2. Place sliced cucumber in a bowl. Sprinkle with salt and toss to coat.
  3. In a small bowl, mix sugar, rice vinegar, soy sauce, and sesame seeds, if using.
  4. Pour vinegar/soy sauce dressing over cucumbers. Toss to coat.
  5. Refrigerate until ready to eat.
